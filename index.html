Talk

Artificial Intelligence in Medicine

  • Artificial intelligence (AI) is a human cognitive mimicking approach using different formats of clinical data to solve various needs in healthcare, such as automated diagnosis and clinical decision making. In this talk, we would like to introduce how AI may influence contemporary clinical medicine, what AI can and can not achieve, what are challenges of AI in the real clinical setting, and how can we survive in the era of machine intelligence.
